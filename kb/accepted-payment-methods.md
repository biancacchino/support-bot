# Accepted payment methods

## What we accept

| Method | Available to | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Visa and Mastercard, debit or credit | All accounts | Credit cards carry no surcharge |
| American Express | All accounts | |
| PayPal | All accounts | |
| Bank transfer (BACS) | All accounts | Order ships once funds clear, 2 to 3 working days |
| Direct debit | Credit-terms accounts | Collected against the monthly statement |
| 30-day credit terms | Approved accounts only | Separate application |
| Account credit | All accounts | Applied automatically at checkout |

We do not accept cheques, cash on delivery, or payment to the driver.

## Cards

Payment is **authorised** when you place the order and **captured** when the order is dispatched.

This means you will see a pending charge straight away but the money does not actually leave your account until the goods ship.
An order placed for delivery next week shows as pending for several days, and that is correct behaviour rather than a stuck payment.

If your order is cancelled before dispatch, the authorisation is released rather than refunded.
Released authorisations disappear from your statement in 3 to 5 working days without ever becoming a real charge.
There is nothing for us to refund, because we never took the money.

## Bank transfer

Choose **Bank transfer** at checkout and you get our bank details and a payment reference.

**Use the reference.**
Without it we cannot match your payment to your order, and the order sits unshipped until somebody works it out manually.

The order is held at **On hold** until the funds clear, which is 2 to 3 working days.
Bank transfer is therefore the wrong choice if you need goods quickly.

## Account credit

Credit on your account, whether from a refund or a goodwill gesture, is applied automatically at checkout before any other payment method.

If your credit covers the whole order, no other payment is needed.
If it partially covers it, you pay the balance by another method.

## 30-day terms

Approved accounts do not pay at checkout.
Orders are placed against your credit limit and invoiced on a monthly statement, payable 30 days from statement date.

If an order would take you over your credit limit it goes **On hold** rather than being rejected.
Settle some of the balance or ask us to review the limit, and it releases.

Terms require a separate application with two trade references and a credit check, and take around 5 working days to assess.

## Changing how you pay

You cannot change the payment method on an order you have already placed.
Cancel it before picking starts, which is free, and place it again with the method you want.
