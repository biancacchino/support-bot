# Cancelling an order

Whether you can cancel depends entirely on how far the order has progressed.

## Cancelling online

Sign in, go to **Orders**, open the order, and choose **Cancel order**.
If the button is not there, the order has passed the point where it can be cancelled by itself, and you need to talk to us.

## What can be cancelled, and when

| Order status | Can you cancel? |
|---|---|
| Confirmed | Yes, immediately and in full, at no charge |
| Being picked | Yes, but a cancellation fee may apply |
| Dispatched | No. It has to be refused on delivery or returned |
| Delivered | No. This becomes a return or a refund |

The practical cut-off is when picking begins.
For ambient goods that is typically 6pm the day before delivery.
For chilled and frozen goods it is 2pm the day before, because those orders are picked and packed earlier.

## Cancelling part of an order

You can cancel individual lines rather than the whole order, using **Cancel item** against the line.

Be aware that dropping lines can take the order below the minimum order value for your account.
If it does, you have to either cancel the whole order or add something else to it.
The system tells you at the point of cancelling rather than letting you discover it later.

## Cancelling after dispatch

Once the order is with the courier we cannot recall it.
You have two options:

- **Refuse the delivery.** Tell the driver you are rejecting the consignment. It comes back to us and is refunded in full, with no cancellation fee.
- **Accept it and return it.** This follows the returns process and the refund policy, which are different from cancellation and have their own conditions.

Refusing is much simpler than returning, so if you know you no longer want the goods and they have not yet arrived, refusing on the doorstep is the cleanest option.

## Perishable and special-order goods

Chilled, frozen, and fresh lines cannot be cancelled once picked, because they cannot be returned to saleable stock once they have left the cold store.

Special orders and imported seasonal lines that we buy in specifically for you cannot be cancelled once we have placed the order with the producer.
You are told at checkout when a line falls into this category.

## Will you be charged?

Cancelling before picking is always free.
Cancelling after picking has begun may carry a fee.
See the article on cancellation fees for the amounts and how they are calculated.
