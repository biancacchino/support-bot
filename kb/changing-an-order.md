# Changing an order

You can change quantities, add lines, remove lines, or move the delivery date, as long as the order has not started being picked.

## Making a change

Sign in, go to **Orders**, open the order, and choose **Edit order**.

If **Edit order** is greyed out, picking has started and the order is locked.
At that point your options are to cancel it, refuse it on delivery, or accept it and return what you do not want.

## The cut-off

The order locks when picking begins:

- **Ambient goods:** 6pm the day before the delivery date.
- **Chilled and frozen goods:** 2pm the day before the delivery date.

An order containing both takes the earlier cut-off, so a mixed order locks at 2pm.

## What you can change

**Quantities.** Increase or decrease freely before the cut-off. Increasing is subject to stock being available at the time you make the change, not at the time you originally ordered.

**Adding lines.** You can add to an existing order rather than placing a second one, which avoids paying delivery twice. Adding a chilled line to an ambient order moves the whole order to the earlier 2pm cut-off.

**Removing lines.** Removing lines can take the order below your minimum order value. If it does, you either add something else or cancel the order outright.

**Delivery date.** You can move the date to any available slot. Moving it resets the cut-off to the new date, so an order you had locked can become editable again by pushing the date back.

**Delivery address.** Handled separately. See the article on changing your shipping address, because address changes have different rules and a later cut-off.

## What you cannot change

You cannot change the payment method on an existing order.
To pay a different way, cancel the order before picking and place it again.

You cannot convert a standard order into a special order, or the reverse.

You cannot change an order that has been part-dispatched, even for the lines that have not yet gone.

## Price changes

If you edit an order, the lines you add are priced at the current price, not the price when you originally ordered.
Lines you do not touch keep their original price.

This matters on promotional lines: if a promotion has ended between ordering and editing, re-adding a line you removed will re-add it at full price.
The basket shows you the price it will use before you confirm.
