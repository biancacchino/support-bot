# Delivery options and costs

## The options

| Option | When it arrives | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 2 to 3 working days | £7.50, free over £350 |
| Named day | The working day you choose | £9.50 |
| Next day | Next working day, ordered before the cut-off | £14.50 |
| Pre-9am | Next working day before 9am | £24.50 |
| Pallet | 3 to 5 working days, for orders over 500kg | Quoted per order |
| Collection | Same day, from our Reading depot | Free |

All prices exclude VAT.
The option you can choose depends on your postcode and on what is in the basket, so not every option appears on every order.

## Free delivery

Standard delivery is free on orders over **£350** excluding VAT.

The threshold is on goods value, not the invoice total, so VAT and the delivery charge itself do not count towards it.
An order of £340 plus VAT does not qualify, even though the invoice is over £350.

Free delivery applies to standard only.
Choosing a faster option on a large order still costs the full price of that option, rather than the difference.

## Chilled and frozen

Temperature-controlled lines cannot go by standard delivery.
They ship on **named day** or faster, and they carry a **£6.00** cold-chain surcharge per consignment, which covers the insulated packaging and coolant.

The surcharge is per consignment, not per line, so consolidating chilled goods into one order rather than three saves £12.

A basket with both ambient and chilled goods ships as one consignment where possible.
Where it is not, it part-dispatches, and you are only charged one delivery fee regardless.

## Zones

Prices above are mainland UK.

- **Scottish Highlands, and offshore including the Isle of Wight, Isle of Man, and Channel Islands:** add £12.00, and add one working day.
- **Northern Ireland:** add £15.00, add one working day, and note that chilled and frozen lines are not available.
- **Islands without a daily ferry:** ambient only. We do not ship perishables where we cannot guarantee the cold chain, and no surcharge will change that.

The checkout shows the real price for your postcode before you pay.

## Collection

Collect free from our Reading depot, Monday to Friday, 7am to 4pm.

Order online, choose **Collection**, and wait for the ready-to-collect email before travelling.
Turning up before it arrives means waiting, since the order will not have been picked.

Bring the order number and photo ID.

## Pallet deliveries

Orders over 500kg go by pallet, and the price is quoted per order rather than fixed.

Pallet deliveries need somewhere a pallet can actually be received: a tail-lift vehicle needs space, and the driver will not break down a pallet or carry goods inside.
Tell us at checkout if the site has no loading bay, because a pallet that cannot be unloaded comes back, and that is charged as a failed delivery.
