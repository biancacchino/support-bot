# Order statuses explained

The status on your Orders page determines what you can still do with an order, so it is worth knowing what each one means.

## The statuses

**Confirmed.**
We have the order and payment is authorised, but nothing has been picked.
This is the flexible state: you can edit it, cancel it, or change the address, all at no charge.

**Being picked.**
Someone is physically assembling the order in the warehouse.
The order is now locked. You cannot edit it, and cancelling it may carry a fee.

**Packed.**
The order is assembled, boxed, and waiting for the courier.
For chilled and frozen goods this is when it goes into temperature-controlled packaging.

**Dispatched.**
The consignment is with the courier and a tracking link is available.
We cannot recall it. If you no longer want it, refuse it at the door.

**Out for delivery.**
It is on the vehicle and arriving today.

**Delivered.**
Signed for. Any problem from this point is a return, a refund, or a damage claim rather than a cancellation.

**Part-dispatched.**
Some lines have gone and some have not, usually because a line was out of stock or a chilled line is following on a separate vehicle.
The lines still to come keep their own status.

**Cancelled.**
The order is dead. Any payment taken is refunded.

**On hold.**
Something needs resolving before we can proceed, most often a payment authorisation that failed or a credit limit that has been reached.
An order on hold is not progressing, so if you see this, act on it.
We email you when an order goes on hold, but that email is easy to miss.

## Why the status matters

Almost every question in this help centre resolves to the status:

- Can I still change it? Only if it is **Confirmed**.
- Will I be charged to cancel? Only if it is **Being picked** or later.
- Where is it? Tracking exists from **Dispatched** onwards.
- Why has nothing happened? Check for **On hold**.

## Statuses that seem stuck

**Confirmed** for several days is normal if you ordered for a delivery date in the future.
Nothing happens to an order until the day before it is due.

**Packed** overnight is normal.
Orders are packed in the evening and collected the following morning.

**On hold** never resolves itself.
It always needs either you or us to do something, so it is the one status you should never wait out.
