# Placing an order

You can order from Northwind Traders through your online account, by phone with your account manager, or by emailing a purchase order if your business is on approved terms.

## Ordering online

Sign in, add products to your basket, and go to checkout.
At checkout you confirm three things: the delivery address, the delivery option, and the payment method.
Once you place the order you get an order number in the format `NW-` followed by six digits, for example `NW-104517`. Keep it, since every other process in this help centre keys off it.

An order confirmation email arrives within about 15 minutes.
If it has not arrived after an hour, check the spam folder before contacting us, and confirm the address on your account is current.

## Minimum order values

Northwind is a wholesale supplier, so orders have minimums:

- Standard accounts: £150 excluding VAT and delivery.
- Chilled and frozen lines: £250 excluding VAT, because these ship in temperature-controlled packaging that is not economical below that value.
- Accounts on 30-day payment terms: no minimum.

The basket tells you if you are below the minimum for what it contains.

## Stock and availability

Stock levels shown online refresh every 30 minutes, so a fast-moving line can sell out between you adding it to the basket and checking out.
If that happens, the item is dropped from the order and you are not charged for it.
You will see this on the confirmation email as a short-shipped line.

Seasonal and imported lines are marked with an expected availability date rather than a stock count.
Ordering these places a reservation: you are charged when the goods ship, not when you order.

## Ordering by phone or purchase order

Accounts with a named account manager can order by phone on 0800 496 1234, Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm.

If your business is on approved credit terms you can email a purchase order to orders@northwind.example.
Include your account number and a delivery date.
Purchase orders are acknowledged within one working day, and the acknowledgement is what confirms the order, not the email you send.

## After you order

Once an order is placed you can still track it, change it, or cancel it, subject to how far it has progressed.
See the separate articles on tracking, changing, and cancelling orders.
