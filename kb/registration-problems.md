# Registration problems

Common reasons sign-up fails, and what to do about each.

## The confirmation email never arrived

This is by far the most common problem, and it is nearly always one of three things.

**Check spam and junk.** Automated mail from a new sender frequently lands there.

**Check the address you typed.** The sign-up form does not ask you to confirm your email twice, so a typo goes unnoticed. If you typed it wrongly, just sign up again with the correct address. The half-created account expires on its own after 24 hours and does not block you.

**Check with whoever runs your mail.** Business mail servers sometimes quarantine mail from unfamiliar domains before it reaches an inbox at all. Ask them to release anything from `northwind.example`.

You can trigger a new confirmation email from the sign-in page by entering your address and choosing **Resend confirmation**.
The new link invalidates the old one, so if you later find the first email, that link will not work.

## "An account already exists with this email"

Someone at your business has probably already registered, or you signed up previously and forgot.

Use **Forgot password** rather than creating a new account.
If the reset email goes to someone who has left the business, contact us and we will transfer the account.

We will not simply hand over an existing business account to a new email address without checking, because that is exactly what an account takeover looks like.
Expect to be asked to confirm details we already hold.

## The VAT number is rejected

The number is validated against the official VIES service in real time, and that service is occasionally down.

Check the number is right, with no spaces and with the country prefix.
If it is right and still fails, wait an hour and try again, since it is usually the validation service rather than your number.

You can also complete sign-up without a VAT number and add it later from account settings.
This does not disadvantage you, other than that VAT-registered pricing will not show until it is verified.

## The business name does not match

We check against Companies House.
Trading names frequently differ from registered names, and that is fine.

Enter the **registered** name in the business name field and put your trading name in the trading-as field.
If you are a sole trader with no company number, leave the company number blank rather than inventing one.

## Verification is taking too long

Manual verification is up to 2 working days.
If it has been longer than that, it has probably stalled waiting for something.

Check your email for a request for more information, including spam.
If there is nothing, contact us with the business name you registered under.

## Residential delivery address

An account with a residential delivery address will always go to manual verification, and may be refused.

If you genuinely trade from a residential address, say so when we ask.
It is not a bar to opening an account, it just cannot be approved automatically.
