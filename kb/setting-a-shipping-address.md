# Setting up a shipping address

## Adding an address

Sign in, go to **Account settings**, then **Addresses**, and choose **Add address**.

You can hold as many delivery addresses as you have sites.
At checkout you pick which one the order goes to, and the last one you used is preselected.

## What we need

- A full address with postcode.
- **A contact name and mobile number for that site.** The courier uses this, not your account contact. Get it wrong and the driver has nobody to call when they cannot find the loading bay.
- The hours somebody is there to receive goods.
- Access notes: which entrance, whether there is a loading bay, height restrictions, whether the driver needs to book in.

The access notes matter more than they look.
A failed delivery because the driver could not get a 7.5-tonne vehicle into a courtyard is charged as a failed delivery, and for chilled goods it usually means the consignment is destroyed.

## Delivery windows

Set the hours the site can actually receive goods.

If nobody is there before 9am, say so.
Chilled and frozen consignments are delivered early precisely because they are temperature-sensitive, so a site that cannot receive before 9am needs us to know that up front rather than discovering it on the doorstep.

Addresses with no delivery window are treated as available 8am to 5pm, Monday to Friday.

## Billing address is separate

The address on your invoice is your **billing** address, held in **Account settings**, and it is not the same field as your delivery addresses.

Changing a delivery address does not change where invoices go, and vice versa.
People frequently change one and expect the other to follow.

## Verification

New addresses are usable straight away for card and PayPal orders.

Addresses on **credit-terms** accounts are verified before first use, because a new delivery address on a credit account is a classic fraud pattern: goods on your credit, delivered somewhere you have never shipped to.
Verification takes up to 1 working day, and we may call the number on the account.

## Residential addresses

We can deliver to a residential address but it is worth knowing what it means.

Vehicles are large, deliveries are palletised for bigger orders, and the driver will not carry goods inside.
Chilled and frozen deliveries arrive early and cannot be left.

If nobody is in, a perishable consignment cannot be redelivered and is destroyed, and it is not refundable, because the failure was at your end.

## Which address an order uses

The address is fixed at checkout, not at dispatch.

Changing a stored address does **not** change where an existing order is going.
To redirect an order you have already placed, see the article on changing your shipping address.
