# Tracking your order

Every Northwind order can be tracked from the moment it is confirmed until it is signed for.

## Where to track

Sign in and go to **Orders**.
Each order shows its current status, the last time that status changed, and a tracking link once the order has left our warehouse.

You do not need to sign in to use the tracking link in your dispatch email.
That link works for 60 days after dispatch, then expires.

## What you need

Tracking is keyed on your order number, in the format `NW-104517`.
It is on your order confirmation email, your dispatch email, and the Orders page.

If you ordered by purchase order rather than online, the order number is on the acknowledgement we sent you, not on the purchase order you sent us.

## When tracking information appears

The courier tracking link is created when we hand the consignment over, which is usually the evening before delivery.
Between placing the order and that handover, the order status on the Orders page is the only tracking available, and that is expected rather than a fault.

Chilled and frozen consignments are handed over on the morning of delivery rather than the night before, because they are packed last to protect the cold chain.
Tracking for these appears later in the process, typically between 5am and 7am on the delivery day.

## If tracking has not updated

Courier scans can lag by several hours, particularly overnight and at weekends.
A tracking page that has not moved for less than 24 hours during a working week is normally still in transit.

Treat it as a problem if:

- Tracking has not updated for more than one full working day.
- Tracking says delivered but nothing has arrived.
- The delivery date has passed with no tracking activity at all.

In any of those cases the order needs a human to chase the courier, and you should contact us with the order number.

## Deliveries marked as delivered but missing

Check first with anyone else who might have signed for it, and look for a card or a note from the driver.
Couriers sometimes leave consignments with a neighbouring unit on a trading estate.

If it is genuinely missing, tell us within 3 working days of the delivery scan.
After that the courier's own claims window closes and it becomes much harder to recover.
