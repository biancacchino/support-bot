# Updating your account details

Most details you can change yourself.
A few need us to verify them first, because they affect pricing, invoicing, or credit.

## What you can change yourself

Sign in and go to **Account settings**.

- Contact name and phone number.
- Email address used for sign-in.
- Notification preferences, including who receives order confirmations and dispatch emails.
- Delivery addresses, covered in more detail in the shipping address articles.
- Password.

These take effect immediately.

## What needs verification

Some changes go to us for review before they take effect, and the account keeps working on the old details in the meantime.

**Registered business name.** Checked against Companies House.

**VAT number.** Validated against VIES. Adding or changing this changes how you are invoiced, so it cannot be self-served.

**Billing address.** Affects invoicing and, if you are on credit terms, your credit file.

**Bank details for refunds and direct debit.** Always verified, always by a human, and we will call you on the number we already hold to confirm it. We will never accept a change of bank details by email alone. This is the single most common route for invoice fraud, and the callback is not negotiable.

Verification takes up to 2 working days.

## Changing the sign-in email

Change it in **Account settings**.
A confirmation link goes to the **new** address, and a notification goes to the old one so that an unauthorised change is visible to whoever previously held the account.

The change does not take effect until you click the link in the new address.
If you cannot receive mail at the new address, the change will silently never complete, so make sure the address works before you change it.

## Adding and removing users

Business accounts can have several users.
The account owner can invite users from **Account settings**, then **Users**.

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| Owner | Everything, including billing, credit, and removing users |
| Buyer | Place, edit, and cancel orders. Cannot see invoices or change account details |
| Finance | See invoices and statements, raise refunds. Cannot place orders |

**Remove users the day they leave.**
A buyer account left active is a live route to order goods on your credit, and it is the most common way trade accounts get abused.

## Transferring ownership

If the account owner leaves, ownership can be transferred to another user on the account.
The current owner can do this themselves from **Users**.

If the owner has already gone and cannot, contact us.
We will need confirmation from a director or from a verifiable company address, for the same reasons set out in the password article.
